This Week's Headlines

A Complete Onboarding Programme — Built, Deployed & Ready for Review

What began as a blank Zoho Learn space is now a fully operational three-day onboarding system for Alpha Hippo Connectors — the most complete training resource this team has ever had.

Fifty-three modules. Three full days of structured learning. Six phases of call flow. Twenty-eight objections scripted and built into interactive training. Two real call recordings embedded with guided analysis. This week, the Alpha Hippo onboarding programme reached structural completion.

Day 1 covers company foundation — who RESINC is, how solar works, what separates Alpha Hippo from every other setter team. Day 2 covers role and performance — KPIs, coaching, career progression, what a focused day looks like. Day 3 covers systems and scripts — dialer workflow, Zoho CRM, the full call flow, and the DTRC objection framework applied to real scenarios.

Every module was purpose-built as a single-page interactive HTML experience, deployed via GitHub Pages, and embedded in Zoho Learn via iframe — no SCORM, no off-the-shelf templates, no recycled content.
